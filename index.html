Welcome to Northeast India's Green Heritage

Northeast India is one of the richest biodiversity hotspots in the world. The region is home to numerous horticultural crops and medicinal plants that are valuable for agriculture, healthcare, and economic development.

Importance of Medicinal Plants

Medicinal plants play a vital role in Ayurveda, traditional healing, modern pharmaceuticals, and rural healthcare. Northeast India possesses hundreds of medicinal species that contribute to biodiversity conservation, sustainable agriculture, and livelihood generation.
